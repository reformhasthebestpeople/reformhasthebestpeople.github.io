Gary Raikes' Facebook Friends

June 11, 2024
far-right

The Times reports that at least 41 people standing for Reform are connected Gary Raikes on Facebook.

Raikes, a former organiser for the British National Party, founded the New British Union in the image of Oswald Mosley’s British Union of Fascists, with activists who call themselves “blackshirts”. The New British Union has called for a “fascist revolution” and sees parliamentary democracy as an “obstruction” to be replaced with a dictatorship.

Of the 41 Reform candidates linked to Raikes as Facebook Friends, The Times named the following:

NameConstituency
Steve BeattyDidcot and Wantage
Leslie LilleySouthend East and Rochford
Grant RandallRayleigh and Wickford

Leslie Lilley, candidate for Southend East & Rochford is also reported as posting messages suggesting that “the Illuminati” control the world and that the Covid-19 vaccine rollout was a “crime against humanity”.
