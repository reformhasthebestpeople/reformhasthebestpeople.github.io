Angela Carter-Begbie calls King Charles 'weak'

June 18, 2024
misc

Showing that it has a knack for discovering questionable Reform candidates, The Times reports on Angela Carter-Begbie, who has made some ‘interesting’ comments on Twitter/X.

In a reply to a deleted post, she described King Charles and Parliament as “weak” and implied he was under the control of global elites. The Queen’s Park and Maida Vale candidate also questioned the King’s loyalty to Britain, at least as far as you call tell from her post:

Just listen about th #proms I will b honest I’m not afan of King Charles. But 2 do th flags @ th #proms how disrespectful of the #BBC 2allow this 2 happen 2 OUR King,in away it is King Charles fault 4 supporting the WEF Think it’s about time King Charles show where he truly lye🇬🇧

In another post from May last year, she referred to King Charles as a ‘WEF muppet. A king should not be a muppet but he is’.

Carter-Begbie frequently posts about the World Economic Forum, apparently believing that the organisation is part of a globalist plot to control, among other things, the EU and both the Labour and Conservative parties. This has been a running theme among many Reform supporters, culminating in Nigel Farage claiming that a Reform government would cancel Britain’s membership of the WEF, despite not being a member…
