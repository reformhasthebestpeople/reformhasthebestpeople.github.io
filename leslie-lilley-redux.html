Leslie Lilley said he would ‘slaughter migrants’

June 27, 2024
anti-vax immigration

Leslie Lilley, not content with being outed for his links to Gary Raikes, has also made worrying statements on Facebook about refugees. The Southend East & Rochford candidate made the comments on his official Facebook campaign account.

In June 2020, Lilley responded to news of a small boat arriving in Dover by posting: “I hope I’m near one of these scumbags one day I won’t run away I’ll slaughter them then have their family taken out.” He also suggested Border Force vessels should have razor wire to tear small boats carrying migrants across the Channel.

Lilley has also argued that the pandemic was a plan to “depopulate the world” and was “mass murder by government”.
