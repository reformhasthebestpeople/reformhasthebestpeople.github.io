Lee Anderson wants refugees sent to the Outer Hebrides

May 28, 2024
immigration

28 May, 2024 | Ashfield Constituency

Ex-Labour, ex-Conservative Reform candidate for Ashfield has a solution for dealing with asylum seekers that doesn’t involve sending them to Rwanda. According to Lee Anderson, speaking to Jo Coburn on the BBC’s Politics Live,

… there is another solution to the illegal migrants, and this is going to probably get me into some sort of hot water with you lot in the media.
I said this to a previous home secretary, I won’t say which one, but if some of these migrants do land on the beaches, let’s take them to a remote Scottish island somewhere, the Outer Hebrides where nobody lives, give them the building material and let them set their own camps up.
I’m sure they’ll be safe and free from persecution in war-torn France.”
