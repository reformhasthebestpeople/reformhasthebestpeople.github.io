Ian Gribbin's remarks on Hitler

June 10, 2024
far-right misogyny putin

Bexhill and Battle Constituency

Ian Gribbin, candidate in Bexhill and Battle, has apologised for posts he made on the Unherd website, including the claim that Britain would be “in a far better state today” if it had “taken Hitler up on his offer of neutrality” instead of fighting the Nazis in World War Two.

Gribbin also wrote that women were the “sponging gender” and should be “deprived of health care”.

Men pay 80% of tax – women spend 80% of tax revenue. On aggregate as a group you only take from society.

Just to cap things off, Gribbin also said Winston Churchill was “abysmal” and praised Russian President Vladimir Putin.

The Reform Party’s initial response to criticisms of the remarks was to defend them, including telling the Jewish Chronicle, “We’re not going to sack him, he’s done nothing wrong.”

After initially declining to comment, Gribbin did later apologise:

I apologise for these old comments and withdraw them unreservedly and the upset that they have caused.”

Commenting on his candidate’s remarks, Nigel Farage claimed vetting issues caused by the unexpected election timing.
